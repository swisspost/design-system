Added scroll buttons to `post-tabs` to allow navigating overflowing tab items. When the tabs container overflows horizontally, previous and next buttons appear to scroll through the available tabs. The buttons are labelled via the required `text-prev-tab-items` and `text-next-tab-items` props, which default to `"Previous tab items"` and `"Next tab items"` respectively.
